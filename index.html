GT Gamefest Is Here.

Gamefest bring together the Tech, Atlanta, and Southeast gaming and eSports communities for a semesterly LAN party of epic proportions. With over 800 attendees from all over the region, Gamefest represents one of the largest gaming experiences the Southeast sees all year.

It is free to come hang out for free play and $15 to particpate in a competitive tournament.
