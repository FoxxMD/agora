Bracket-Style

Double elimination, best of 3. Winners’ Bracket Finals, Losers’ Bracket Finals, and Grand Finals are best of 5.

Rules

Slightly modified Apex 2014 Ruleset will be used for this tournament.

General Rules

  • 3 Stocks, 8 Minute Time Limit

  • Items set to “None” and “Off,” Pause set to “Off.” If you accidentally pause during a match, call an admin. Do not unpause until the admin arrives.

  • Double elimination bracket. All sets are best of 3 except Winners’ Finals, Losers’ Finals, and Grand Finals (best of 5)

  • All infinites and chain grabs are legal (except MK’s Infinite Dimensional Cape)

  • There is a 35 Ledge Grab Limit (LGL) for all characters. This limit only applies in the event that a game goes to time - If one player exceeds the LGL, that player loses. If both players exceed the LGL, this rule has no effect.

  • Stalling in any form is banned. This includes but is not limited to planking (ledge stalling), scrooging (travelling under the stage more than once from the ledge), continuing infinites or chaingrabs past 300%.

  • If the game goes to time and the LGL does not apply, the winner will be determined by stock and then percent. If both players die at the same time or have the same stock and percent, a 1 stock, 3 minute rematch will be played on the same stage, with a LGL of 15. If the game ends via suicide move, the initiator of the move wins.

  • No coaching during the match.

  • Do not be late to your match. 10 minutes late = match loss. 15 minutes late = set loss.

  • Bring your own controller. Ask an admin if you need to borrow one. Desync your Wii Remote after your match (you can do this by removing the batteries). If you disrupt someone else's match with your controller you may be disqualified.

  • No hacks are allowed in tournament matches.

  • Doubles only: Team attack set to “On”; double MK is banned; life stealing/stock sharing is allowed. Players with PT, Lucario, or Sonic may request color changes to easily differentiate between teammates. LGL still applies.

Set Procedure

  1. Each team selects their character. Double blind pick may be called by either team.

  2. Game 1 is played on a stage selected from the Starter List either by mutual consent or by stage striking (Team 1 strikes 1 stage, Team 2 strikes 2 stages, Team 1 strikes 1 stage)

  3. Game 1 is played.

  4. The winner of the previous match may announce one Stage Ban if they have not done so this set.

  5. The loser of the previous match selects and announces the stage for the next match (from the Starter or Counterpick list). No stage announced as a Stage Ban or on which a team has already won in the current set may be selected unless both teams agree to the stage.

  6. The winner of the previous match selects their character(s).

  7. The loser of the previous match selects their character(s).

  8. The next match is played.

  9. Repeat steps 4-8 for all subsequent matches.
