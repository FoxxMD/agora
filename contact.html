GT Gamefest

Celebrating Georgia Tech's geeky roots the only way we could think of.

Gamefest bring together the Tech, Atlanta, and Southeast community of gamers for an annual LAN party of epic proportions. With over 700 attendees from all over the region Gamefest represents one of the largest gaming experience the southeast sees all year.
