GT Gamefest Is Here.

November 16th-17th.

GT Gamefest brings together the Tech, Atlanta, and Southeast gaming and eSports communities for a semesterly LAN event of epic proportions. With over 800 attendees from all over the region, Gamefest represents one of the largest gaming experiences in the Southeast.

Free to attend and play, $15 to compete.

You do not need to be a member of the Georgia Tech community to attend, but you must be physically present to participate. This is a bring-your-own-computer (and Ethernet cable!) event.
