Game Rules

Ground Rules

  • Each participant must follow the instructions from Admins and Sudo, security guards, GTPD and any emergency personnel (even if these instructions go against the tournament rules.)
  • Alcohol and drugs in any form are not allowed. Intoxicated and unruly individuals will not be allowed onto the premises.
  • You may not bring any high watt consuming or heat producing appliances.
  • Do not remove furniture from room without admin approval.
  • Notify Admins about damages and/or malfunctions.
  • Throw all trash into proper disposal containers, notify admins of spills and other dangers.
  • All connected computers and devices will abide by the Georgia Tech Computer and Network Usage and Security Policy http://lawn.gatech.edu/policy/index.html

Tournament General Rules

  • All players must be physically present to advance to finals and be awarded prizes.
  • Bring all disputes, questions and technical issues to Admins.
  • Admins (not staff) have final say, rules subject to change.
  • Teams will be allowed to substitute players only with admin approval.
  • All team members must have registered and paid before joining the tournament.
  • All teams must notify the admin of attendance. Team leaders are responsible of making sure his team is in the bracket at the start of the tournament.
