Counterstrike: Global Offensive

Counterstrike returns this semster with the rise of Counterstrike: Global Offensive as a popular competitive event. CS:GO is an online first-person shooter developed by Valve Corporation and Hidden Path Entertainment. Global Offensive is an objective-based multiplayer first-person shooter. Terrorists must plant a C4 explosive at one of two designated bombsites; the Counter-Terrorists must prevent the bomb from exploding, either ensuring that the terrorist team does not plant it or defusing it once it is activated.
